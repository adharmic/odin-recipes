Corn Pudding

Description

This creamy corn casserole is easy to make with both whole kernel and cream-style corn, resulting in a custardy pudding that will be a welcome addition to your holiday table.

Ingredients

  • Eggs
  • Butter
  • White Sugar
  • Milk
  • Cornstarch
  • Whole Kernel Corn
  • Cream-Style Corn

Steps

  1. Preheat oven and grease casserole dish
  2. Whisk eggs and add milk, melted butter, sugar, and cornstarch
  3. Stir in corn and whisk until blended
  4. Pour mixture into casserole dish
  5. Bake until golden brown
