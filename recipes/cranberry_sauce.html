Cranberry Sauce

Description

Skip the store-bought stuff this year and serve homemade cranberry sauce. It's actually way easier than you think.

Ingredients

  • Fresh Cranberries
  • Sugar
  • Orange Juice

Steps

  1. Heat orange juice in a saucepan
  2. Stir in sugar and cook until dissolved
  3. Add cranberries and continue stirring until they pop
  4. Remove from heat and let thicken until cooled
